- License: Various Creative Commons
- Quality: High
- Use: Download 5-10 tracks for demo

### 2. Incompetech (incompetech.com)
- Artist: Kevin MacLeod
- License: CC BY 4.0 (just credit him)
- Quality: Professional
- Use: Great for consistent quality
