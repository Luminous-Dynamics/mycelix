About ZK Tax Proofs

Zero-knowledge proofs allow you to prove statements about your financial data without revealing the underlying information. This is revolutionary for privacy-preserving compliance.

Privacy First

Your actual income is NEVER revealed. The proof only shows which bracket you fall into, not your exact earnings.

Cryptographically Secure

Built on STARK proofs via Risc0 zkVM. Mathematical guarantees that proofs cannot be forged.

19 Jurisdictions

Support for all G20 countries including US, UK, EU, Japan, China, India, and more.

Historical Data

Tax brackets from 2020-2025 for multi-year compliance verification.

Range Proofs

Prove income is above a minimum (loans) or below a maximum (benefits) without exact disclosure.

Holochain Ready

Integrated with Mycelix DHT for decentralized proof storage and verification.
