## Key Findings

### 1. Training Effectiveness

**Results mixed.** Less than 50% of participants showed improvement across all methods.

### 2. Best Method

The most effective training method was **BaseRateAnchor**, achieving an average ECE improvement of 0.0308.

### 3. Persistence

Calibration improvements have a half-life of approximately 1000 steps, suggesting that:
- Regular practice is needed to maintain calibration gains
- Booster training sessions may be beneficial
- Integration into daily forecasting workflows is recommended

### 4. Cross-Domain Transfer

**Limited transfer** between domains. Domain-specific training recommended.
