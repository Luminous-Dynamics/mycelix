A Paradigm Shift for Science

From Panels to Quadratic Funding

Mycelix replaces closed review panels with a community-driven model. Small donations from many scientists are quadratically matched by a larger fund, amplifying the collective intelligence of the community and funding a wider range of innovative ideas.

From Prestige to Verifiable Reputation

Reputation is no longer based on institutional prestige. Instead, it's a dynamic, multi-dimensional score based on verifiable on-chain contributions, creating a true meritocracy.

From Static PDFs to "Living Papers"

Scientific papers become living, executable objects. They contain the text, raw data, analysis code, and a cryptographic hash of the results, making science transparent and reproducible by default.

Hybrid Architecture Benefits

By combining a P2P layer for scalability with a Layer-2 blockchain for settlement, Mycelix achieves massive performance gains over traditional blockchains, enabling high-frequency scientific data and transactions.

Future-Proof Bridge Scalability

The system is designed to evolve. The initial bridge architecture already enables significant throughput, with a clear upgrade path to a ZK-Bridge that offers a 100x improvement, ensuring long-term viability.
