Scalability & Performance Analysis

The architecture is designed to avoid bottlenecks. Most activity occurs on Holochain's horizontally scaling P2P layer, with only final settlement crossing the bridge to the L2.

Holochain's agent-centric design allows for thousands of TPS per application, while the ZK-Bridge eliminates the settlement layer as a bottleneck.

Deployment Roadmap

Mycelix follows a phased deployment, beginning with a robust and tested production-ready architecture, while actively developing the next generation of trustless infrastructure.

Phase 1: Verifiable Bridge (Current)

Production deployment on Polygon PoS. Security model based on staked validators, economic slashing, and on-chain Merkle proof verification. Architecture complete and ready for mainnet.

Phase 2: ZK-Bridge R&D (Active)

Parallel research and development into ZK-SNARK provers and Circom circuits. The goal is to build a trustless ZK-Rollup architecture to replace the validator network.

Phase 2.5: Gradual Migration

A planned, gradual migration path from Phase 1 validators to Phase 2 provers, ensuring backward compatibility and a seamless transition for the network and its users.

Perfection: The Trust Layer

The ultimate evolution of the bridge to a state of maximal security, privacy, and efficiency, where all cross-chain state transitions are guaranteed by pure mathematics.
