Combating Misinformation with PoGQ

Mycelix's "Proof of Gradient Quality" (PoGQ) is a novel mechanism for collaborative content verification. It allows the network to validate the authenticity and quality of contributions, forming a powerful defense against AI-generated deepfakes and misinformation.

1. Content Contribution

A creator submits new content (e.g., an article, image, or model update) to the network via their local Holochain agent.

↓

2. Validator Selection

The network uses a Verifiable Random Function (VRF) to select a decentralized set of validators to review the contribution.

↓

3. PoGQ Validation

Validators assess the content against private, high-quality datasets, generating a PoGQ score that measures its authenticity and accuracy.

↓

4. Aggregation & Reputation Update

High-quality, validated contributions are accepted. The contributor's reputation score increases, strengthening the trust layer of the entire network.
