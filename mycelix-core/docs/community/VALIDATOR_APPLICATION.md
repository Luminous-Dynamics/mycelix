## Overview

Thank you for your interest in becoming a Mycelix Genesis Validator! Genesis validators are the foundational infrastructure providers who will launch and secure the Mycelix mainnet.

**Benefits**:
- Genesis validator badge and recognition
- Priority support channel access
- Early access to protocol upgrades
- Foundation grant eligibility
- Governance participation from day one

**Responsibilities**:
- Maintain 99%+ uptime
- Participate in consensus and FL rounds
- Respond to security alerts within 2 hours
- Attend monthly validator calls
- Follow upgrade coordination procedures
