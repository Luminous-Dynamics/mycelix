# Mycelix Civilizational OS: Complete hApp Catalog

## Overview

The Mycelix Civilizational OS comprises **43 hApps** organized into tiers based on their role in the ecosystem. This document provides the complete catalog with design document references.

The ecosystem is grounded in **Behavioral Economics** (Thaler, Kahneman) and **Integral Theory** (Ken Wilber's AQAL) to ensure both practical effectiveness and comprehensive coverage of human development.
