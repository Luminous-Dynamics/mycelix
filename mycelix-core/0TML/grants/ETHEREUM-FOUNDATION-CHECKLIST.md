## 🎓 Tips for Success

### Writing Tips
- **Be concrete, not abstract**: Use specific numbers (1,000+ users, 10+ DAOs) not vague claims ("many users")
- **Show, don't tell**: Include code snippets, architecture diagrams, experimental results
- **Ethereum-first framing**: Always connect back to Ethereum ecosystem benefits
- **Realistic scope**: Don't overpromise (6 months to production testnet, not mainnet)
- **Open source emphasis**: Stress public good nature, permissionless use, no platform lock-in

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Vague timelines ("Q1 2026" instead of "January 15, 2026")
- ❌ Theoretical-only (no working code or experiments)
- ❌ Overly academic language (use accessible explanations)
- ❌ Ignoring risks (address them proactively with mitigations)
- ❌ No clear Ethereum ecosystem integration (must show how Ethereum dApps benefit)

### Competitive Advantages to Emphasize
- ✅ Working prototype (PoGQ mechanism validated experimentally)
- ✅ Ethereum-native design (ERC standards, ENS, Layer-2)
- ✅ Public good mindset (open source, no extraction)
- ✅ Clear ecosystem gaps addressed (Sybil resistance, reputation portability)
- ✅ Realistic 6-month scope (achievable, concrete deliverables)
