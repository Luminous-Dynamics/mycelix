🚀 How to Connect Two Browsers

  1. Open this page in two different browser windows (or on two devices)
  2. Copy your Peer ID from Window 1
  3. Paste it in Window 2's "Remote Peer ID" field and click "Connect"
  4. Copy the "offer" signal from Window 2
  5. Paste it in Window 1's signal input and click "Process"
  6. Copy the "answer" signal from Window 1
  7. Paste it back in Window 2 and click "Process"
  8. You're connected! Start sending messages!
